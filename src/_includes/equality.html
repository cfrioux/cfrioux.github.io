Activities

I try to commit to activities related to gender equality and inclusion in Academia and in particular in computer science.

  • Starting from 2023, I am a member of the national committe for gender equality and inclusion at Inria. I am involved in several working groups: "communication", "preventing harassment and gender-related violence", and I lead the working group "inclusion in the work environment".
  • Starting from 2022, I co-founded and am a member of the local working group for gender equality, diversity and inclusion at Inria Bordeaux.
