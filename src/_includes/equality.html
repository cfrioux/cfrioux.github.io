Activities

I try to commit to activities related to gender equality and inclusion in Academia and in particular in computer science.

  • Starting from 2023, I am a member of the national committee for gender equality and inclusion at Inria. I am involved in several working groups: "communication", "preventing harassment and gender-related violence", and I lead the working group "inclusion in the work environment".
  • Starting from 2022, I co-founded and am a member of the local working group for gender equality, diversity and inclusion at Inria Bordeaux.

I have been participating for the past years to the MIMM (Moi Informaticienne, Moi Mathématicienne) event organized by the computer science and mathematics departments of Univ. Bordeaux to promote computer science and mathematics to high-school girls. More than 60 of them spend a week on the campus, attending conferences, meeting women researchers, and doing practical sessions in computer science and mathematics. Together with Christèle Etchegaray, we designed and animated a workshop on inclusive communication. We provide our material on GitHub (French only).
