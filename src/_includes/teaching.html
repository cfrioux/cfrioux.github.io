Tutorial on Metabolic modelling

I participated to the EBAME workshop on Computational Microbial Ecogenomics in 2023, where I gave a tutorial on metabolic modelling for microbial communities. The tutorial is freely available on Gitlab and includes several of the tools developped in the team. Do not hesitate to raise an issue if something does not work as expected or is unclear.
